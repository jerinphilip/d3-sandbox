Word level comparison - uncertainty metrics

Description

Vanilla belongs to the class of metrics derivable from the model without further modifications - entropy, max probability and the likes.

For epistemic, I'm demonstrating both the expectation and variance, as Wang et. Al seems to have used both. In theory, expectation should end up something like the max-probability only.

[src], [tgt] indicates the source and target. The stuff in colours are hypotheses, and their respective uncertainties (relative) encoded in colors. Colors are analogous to traffic lights. Red is bad, green is good, yellow somewhere in between.
